### Why Bootleg?

You might be asking, "Why not just buy a shirt from the grocery store?"

Here's my take: **Vintage soft drink tees** that are bootlegged or fan-made just have more soul. Official merch is usually stiff, the designs are vetted by twenty lawyers, and they lack humor.

Bootleg shirts (especially these retro styles) are about culture. They acknowledge that we drink this stuff not just because we're thirsty, but because we're tired, happy, sad, or just need a pick-me-up. They allow for jokes—like the raccoons or the dropkick slogans—that a big corporation would never print.

Plus, the **90s style bootleg design** is trending hard right now. It fits perfectly with the resurgence of baggy jeans and dad sneakers.

### FAQ

**How do these fit?**
Most of these are unisex sizing. If you want that oversized, "I stole this from my boyfriend" look, definitely size up one or two sizes. I usually wear a medium but I buy L or XL in the sweatshirts for maximum coziness.

**What's the print quality like?**
Since they are retro/bootleg style, the prints usually have a bit of texture to them. They aren't those thick, plastic-feeling decals that crack after one wash. They tend to be softer, blending into the fabric, which adds to the vintage look.

**Are these good gifts?**
Absolutely. If you have a friend who always has a Diet Coke in their hand, this is a thoughtful (and funny) gift. It shows you pay attention to their weird little habits.

**How should I wash them?**
To keep the graphic looking good, I always wash mine inside out on cold and tumble dry low. Or hang dry if I’m feeling responsible (I rarely am).
